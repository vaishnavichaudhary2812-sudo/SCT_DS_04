# Traffic Accident Pattern Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing traffic accident data to identify patterns and trends associated with weather conditions, road surface conditions, lighting conditions, and time of day. The objective is to uncover factors that contribute to road accidents and visualize accident-related insights through exploratory data analysis.

## Dataset

Traffic Accident Dataset containing information related to accident conditions, weather, road defects, lighting conditions, injuries, crash timing, and contributing factors.

## Objectives

* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
* Analyze accident patterns and trends
* Study the impact of weather conditions on accidents
* Examine road surface and lighting conditions
* Identify peak accident hours and days
* Explore major contributing causes of accidents

## Analysis Performed

* Data Cleaning
* Weather Condition Analysis

* Injury Severity Analysis
* Contributing Factors Analysis
* Correlation Analysis

## Key Findings

* Accident frequency varies significantly across different hours of the day.
* Weather conditions influence accident occurrence patterns.
* Road surface conditions and lighting conditions contribute to accident risks.
* Certain driver-related factors are among the leading causes of traffic accidents.
* Peak traffic periods show a higher concentration of crashes.
